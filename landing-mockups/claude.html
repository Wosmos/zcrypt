How it works

Five steps. None of
them happen on a
server you don't own.

01

Drop a file

Anything, any size. It never leaves your browser as plaintext.

02

Compress

zstd shrinks it before encryption — less to store, less to upload.

03

Encrypt

AES-256-GCM with a key derived from your passphrase. On device.

04

Chunk

Split into ~10 MB pieces. Unrecognizable on their own.

05

Store

Pushed to your GitHub, GitLab, or Hugging Face.
